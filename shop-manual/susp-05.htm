SUSP-05, Rear Sway Bar Removal and Installation


Acrobat Printable Version

Tools

  • Metric Socket / Wrench Set
  • Floor Jack / Jack Stands

Procedure

  1. Raise the vehicle on jack stands.
  2. Removing the rear wheels makes the job a bit easier but is not required.
  3. Remove the two bolts/nuts on either end of the sway bar that attach the sway bar to the drop links.

  4. Remove the bolts (4 - 2 on each side) that hold the sway bar to the torque tube carrier and remove the sway bar from the vehicle.

  5. Installation is the reverse of removal.
