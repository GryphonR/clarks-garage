BODY-14, 944 Turbo Bumper Cover Removal


Acrobat Printable Version

Tools

  • Phillips Head Screwdriver
  • Metric Socket Set
  • Floor Jack and Jack Stands

Procedure

  1. Raise the front of the vehicle on jack stands.
  2. Remove the belly pan.
  3. Remove the trim panel from the bottom of the bumper cover.
  4. Remove the turn signals and fog lights.
  5. Using a 10mm socket and ratchet, remove the bolts located on either side of bumper cover that attach it to the front fenders.
  6. Remove the two bolts near the center of the bumper cover (10mm). They can be located by looking up between the bumper cover and A/C condenser.
  7. Slide the bumper cover off the front of the car.
